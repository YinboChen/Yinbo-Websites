Children Hospital Registeration App

Yinbo Chen

This application is based on LBS to achieve users’ individual needs. It focuses on some books which are hardly to be found on the online book store. Such as some professional books and some books with small user community. This application links the local small bookstores which shares the information to all nearby customers. It also connects the online book selling platform with the local bookstores. It enhances user’s experiences which includes seeking a book and purchasing a book on the local market. It also generates more profits to the local book stores.
