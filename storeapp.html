Two Shopping App

Yinbo Chen

This is a commercial application which contains three main functions. The first one is making money or points from doing some tasks via this app, such as delivering gloceries, starting an online part-time job, etc. The second one is purchasing kinds of stuff and services. For example, shopping on the local market, finding some specialty restaurants nearby, and so on. The third one is a personal account which includes have earned points or numbers of money, have purchased goods and services, and some details about your personal information and related your account.
