Uber Helper

members: Yinbo Chen, Mutian Yan

This is not a real project for marketing. It is an interactive UI for AAC project. In this project, my partner and I imagine a scenario that the Uber driver is not capable of talking to the passenger, so all the interactions rely on this screen/window display around the passenger seat. When the driver arrives at the pickup location, the passenger's name is displayed on the side window so that passenger can know the uber is for him. There are a series of accommodations for the uber passenger to have a better experience, such as choosing music genres, adjusting temperature, charging phones, wanting a water bottle, etc.
